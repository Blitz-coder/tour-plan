What people think about us

Megan Fox

Stayed 18 Nov, 2019

It was very nice hotel with cleanliness. Staff behavior was good and polite. They welcome us very well. Issue was only that Lift was not in working and we were allotted to 3rd floor and amenities articles were in corner of gallery which were giving bad feeling. Breakfast was good and support of the staff was also very nice. Location is not good as per atmosphere, it is very nearby most of the popular places but self location in a narrow street is not good. Overall it was a good experience and could recommend.

Dragon Mao

Stayed 25 Feb, 2020

TWe rested in this hotel for 7 days during a honeymoon trip. What can I say, this is heaven on earth. We began to dream of returning to this hotel on the very first day of our vacation. What makes it perfect? There is only one answer: service. This hotel has PERFECT service. The people working here know their stuff. Here, everyone is in their place and does their job to the maximum. Before that, we rested for a week in another hotel and I can say that Excellence Playa Mujeres was inferior to another hotel in some things - the territory is quite small, the buildings are very simple and already require renovation, the restaurants are small and I would also like to refresh some, insufficient lighting in the rooms and restaurant ... BUT all this is just a trifle compared to the advantages that you get while relaxing here. Rooms. Yes, there is not enough light in the rooms and would like to update the furniture, but the rooms are huge, bright and spacious. Entering such a room, joy begins to overwhelm. Large balcony with table and chairs and bed. Large jacuzzi, huge bathroom. Small wardrobe. And all this regardless of the room category. Most of all we were pleased with the presence of x-box, where you can order food through the room-service. It is very convenient when you are busy with your own business and do not want to see anyone outside.
